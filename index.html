What is SnackForge?

SnackForge is a next-generation food startup using AI and food science to engineer performance nutrition for hikers, skiers, climbers, and endurance athletes.

Our first prototype — the FROSKI Cold-Resistant Energy Bar — is formulated to stay soft in freezing temperatures and fuel long outdoor adventures.

1. Athlete Input

Tell us your activity type, climate, nutrition goals, and dietary needs.

2. AI Formulation

Our AI generates ingredient ratios, macros, and process steps based on nutrition data and sports requirements.

3. Human Refinement

We produce small-batch prototypes and test with real athletes to validate taste, texture, and cold performance.

FROSKI BAR VISUAL

Introducing the FROSKI Bar

A cold-resistant chocolate-chip energy bar engineered for skiing, mountaineering, and winter hiking.

  • ✔ Soft chew at –10°C to –20°C
  • ✔ ~230–250 kcal • Balanced carbs + fats
  • ✔ Mini chocolate chips and maple crunch
  • ✔ Low moisture for shelf stability
