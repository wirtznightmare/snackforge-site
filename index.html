What is SnackForge?

SnackForge combines food science and AI to engineer performance snacks for extreme outdoor conditions. Our first prototype, the FROSKI Cold-Resistant Energy Bar, is designed to stay soft in freezing temperatures while providing high-energy fuel for endurance.

How It Works

1. Athlete Input

Share your activity type, climate, goals, and dietary preferences.

2. AI Formulation

Our AI generates optimized ingredient ratios, macros, and texture for your performance needs.

3. Human Refinement

We produce small-batch prototypes and test with real athletes for taste, texture, and cold performance.

Introducing the FROSKI Bar

The FROSKI Bar is a cold-resistant chocolate-chip energy bar engineered for skiing, mountaineering, and winter hiking.

  • Soft chew at –10°C to –20°C
  • ~230–250 kcal per bar — balanced carbs + fats
  • Mini chocolate chips & maple crunch
  • Low moisture for shelf stability
